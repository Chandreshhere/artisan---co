About Artisan & Co

Crafted by hand,
delivered with heart

Behind every Artisan & Co creation is Manya — a passionate artisan who believes in the beauty of handmade. Each flower, each arrangement, each piece is meticulously crafted with love and an eye for detail that only human hands can achieve.

From delicate handmade blooms to bespoke floral arrangements for weddings, events, and everyday moments — Artisan & Co brings nature's beauty to life through the art of handcraft.
